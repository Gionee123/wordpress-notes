 WordPress Settings ke Andar Ye Options Hote Hain:
1. General Settings
Site Title – Aapki website ka naam.

Tagline – Ek short description (e.g., "Just another WordPress site").

WordPress Address (URL) – Site ki root URL.

Site Address (URL) – Site kis URL se access hoti hai.

Admin Email Address – Important notifications ke liye.

Membership – Kya koi bhi user register kar sakta hai?

New User Default Role – New users ka default role (Subscriber, Editor, etc.).

Timezone, Date Format, Time Format, Week Starts On – Time aur date ki settings.



2. Writing Settings
Default Post Category – Jab category choose na ho to default.

Default Post Format – (e.g., Standard, Video, Gallery).

Post via Email – Email ke through post karne ka option (rarely used).

Update Services – Jab aap naya post publish karte ho to ping services ko notify karta hai.



3. Reading Settings
Your homepage displays – Aapki homepage kya dikhaye? (Latest posts ya ek static page).

Blog pages show at most – Ek page me kitne posts dikhe.

Syndication feeds show the most recent – RSS feed me kitne items ho.

For each article in a feed, show – Full text ya summary.

Search engine visibility – Aap search engines ko block karna chahte hain ya nahi.



4. Discussion Settings
Default post settings – Comments allow karna ya nahi.

Other comment settings – Name, email zaruri ho ya nahi, auto-close comments, etc.

Email me notifications – Jab koi comment aaye ya hold me ho.

Comment moderation – Kaunse comments approval ke bina show ho.

Blacklist – Specific words/IPs ko block karna.

Avatars – Users ke avatars show karna ya nahi.



5. Media Settings
Thumbnail size, Medium size, Large size – Jab images upload hoti hain to auto resize hoke ye sizes banti hain.

Uploading files – Files kis folder me save hongi.



6. Permalinks Settings
URL structure define karta hai (e.g., example.com/post-name)

Aap apne blog ke URL ko SEO-friendly bana sakte ho.

Plain

Day and name

Month and name

Post name (most used)

Custom structure



7. Privacy Settings
Yahan aap ek Privacy Policy page set kar sakte hain.

WordPress suggest karta hai ki aapke website me privacy policy ho, especially agar aap data collect karte hain.
